SA Founder & Lead Educator
Meet the founder

Teaching built on an engineer's foundation

Sarv Academy was founded on a simple belief: understanding a concept properly, from the ground up, matters more than memorizing steps. That approach comes directly from the founder's own background in engineering and research — where every solution is only as strong as the reasoning underneath it.

Having studied in both Iran and Canada, the founder also understands first-hand what it's like for a student to adjust to a new education system, a new language, and a new way of learning — and brings that patience and perspective into every session.
